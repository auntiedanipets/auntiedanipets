Meet your pet's new auntie

Hi, I'm Dani.

I've always been the person in the neighborhood who everyone trusted with their animals — and honestly, I wouldn't have it any other way. Animals are my people. I understand them, I love them, and I treat every pet I care for like they're my own.

Based right here in Southwest Las Vegas, I offer personalized, one-on-one care so your fur babies get actual attention — not just a quick check-in. When you're away, you'll get photos and updates so you never have to wonder if they're okay.
